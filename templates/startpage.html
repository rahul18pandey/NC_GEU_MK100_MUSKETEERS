Farmer is the backbone of our Nation . Farmer provides us with everything very basic to our need in our life. Without them we our nothing. Through Saksham Kisan we aim to provide them a better price, better market, better services for everything for which they have to spent hours searching around. We provide them help with everything starting from basic to the end selling of product.
